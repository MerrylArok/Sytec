World Class Manufacturing

We manufacture CSA compliant NEMA, IP and UL certified products, from custom-built enclosures to liquid cooled server solutions. Our in-house engineering and manufacturing capabilities lets us control every step of the project, ensuring a product customized and built to specification.

As industry leaders, we are committed to sourcing our materials ethically and responsibly by using Conflict Free Materials (CFM) to ensure that our products do not contribute to human rights abuses. We are also compliant with the Restriction of Hazardous Substances (RoHS) directive to reduce impact on human health and our environment.

World Class Manufacturing

Our in-house engineering and production teams collaborate closely to oversee every detail of our custom fabrication process and guarantee precision-built products tailored exactly to your specifications. We have built long-standing partnerships with industry leaders, as a premier manufacturer of highly specialized CSA (Canadian Standards Association), UL (Underwriters’ Laboratories) & IP (Ingress Protection) rated products.

As industry leaders, we take pride in fostering a safe work enviornment, and in ethical, responsible sourcing of materials. Our products use Conflict-free materials and are compliant with Restriction of Hazardous Substances (RoHS) directive to minimize impact on human health, prevent human rights violations and protect our environment.

Who We Are

Our extensive track record spans across diverse sectors — IT, telecommunications, electrical grid and power distribution, oil and gas, solar & agriculture. This broad expertise equips us with the unique ability to tackle even the most complex and specialized fabrication challenges.

DESIGN

ENCLOSURES

We manufacture custom CSA/ UL Type 1, 12, 3R, 4, 4X enclosures in stainless steel, steel, and aluminum. Enclosures are available as floor stading, wall mounted, or hole mounted with additional features such as door kits, window kits, louvers and custom panels.

TUBE AND PIPE PRODUCTS

We make panel carts, CAT-5 approved wind-loading structures such as shelters and sunshades, to name a few products that utilize our in-house laser cut pipe and tubing.

CABLE MANAGEMENT

We specialize in making IT racks and other diverse cable management products to accommodate a wide range of end-use applications. Standard solutions for 19in EIA products ranging from 1U blanking panels to custom built solutions.

PRODUCT IMPROVEMENT (QICR)

Our team has extensive experience in analyzing and improving existing customer products to extend functionality, enhance usability and reduce manufacturing costs at scale.

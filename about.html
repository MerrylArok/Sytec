ABOUT US

Our foundation is built on promoting a culture of respect, empowerment, and a strong sense of ownership.

Founded in 1998, Sytec Manufacturing Ltd. is a privately held Alberta Corporation. We have deep expertise in advanced metal product design & sheet metal manufacturing, from prototyping designs to rapid production. Our team benefits from the diverse perspectives and rich experiences each team member brings, and we recognize that our collective achievements stem from the dedicated efforts of individuals who choose us as their employer. We are dedicated to nurturing our workforce with a safe, cross-functional work environment that enhances career development and encourages collaboration to drive continuous improvements.

SHEET & TUBE

We work with cold rolled, satin coat and galvanized steel; stainless steel in 304 and 316 grades with a 2B or #4 (brushed) finish; aluminum & copper. Our materials come in various sheet, pipe, channel and tube formats to manufacture our products.

CUSTOM LASER CUTTING

Our advanced Fiber laser can cut and etch various sizes of sheet, pipe, channel & tubing. We can process customer supplied material or provide precise modifications to finished products, such as adding holes to enclosures.

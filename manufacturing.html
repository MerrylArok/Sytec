ENCLOSURES

We make custom enclosures ranging from Type 1 to Type 4X, as well as racks and tophats. Our modular and easy-to-assemble solutions are available as floor-standing or wall-mounted designs in stainless steel, steel, and aluminum. Optional features include door kits, window kits, louvers and custom panels.

TUBE AND PIPE PRODUCTS

We design panel carts, P.Eng authorized CAT 5 wind loading approved structures such as shelters and sunshades, to name a few of our products that utilize our in-house laser cut pipe and tubing.

We specialize in making IT racks and other diverse cable management products to accommodate a wide range of end-use applications. Standard solutions for 19in EIA installation racks ranging from 1U Blanking Panels to custom built solutions.
